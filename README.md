# ISCTE Impact Token

## 🌱 Project Overview
ISCTE Impact Token is a **sustainability and social responsibility concept** developed for the ISCTE Technology & Innovation Ideas Competition.

The project was **selected as a finalist**, reaching the final presentation stage.

It proposes a **token-based reward system** to incentivize sustainable behaviors within the ISCTE academic community, leveraging technology, gamification, and local partnerships.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To encourage and reward sustainable and altruistic actions through a digital ecosystem that:
- Promotes recycling, donations, and volunteering
- Strengthens the local community and small businesses
- Enhances ISCTE’s sustainability impact and visibility

---

## 💡 How It Works
- ISCTE students and staff register using their institutional email
- Users earn **Impact Tokens** by performing one of the following actions:
  - ♻️ Recycling
  - 🤝 Volunteering
  - 👕 Donating second-hand items
- Actions are validated via **QR codes** at partner locations
- Tokens can be exchanged for **discounts at local businesses around the campus**

---

## 📱 Application Concept
The mobile application centralizes:
- Token balance and activity history
- Locations of recycling points and partner institutions
- Rewards and discounts marketplace
- Gamification features (teams, rankings, badges)

App mockups are available in the repository.

## 🧠 Key Features

- Gamification (teams, rankings, badges)
